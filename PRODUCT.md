# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Knowledge managers, analysts, and developers seeking to query, organize, compile, and digest technical/organizational documents using LLMs.

## Product Purpose
To provide an advanced, high-performance web interface for managing knowledge bases, executing semantic queries, performing research, and tracking data ingestion and compilation workflows.

## Brand Personality
- Technical (developer-focused, clean, precise)
- High-Performance (fast loading, responsive, minimal latency)
- Trustworthy (secure, accurate, reliable)

## Anti-references
- SaaS-cream / cookie-cutter warm-neutral templates
- Overly decorated consumer apps with low information density
- Fragmented layout and spacing systems

## Design Principles
- High Information Density: Maximize screen efficiency without cluttering.
- Expert Confidence: Minimalist typography, precise alignment, robust layouts.
- Uncompromised Utility: Clean, clear states (empty, loading, error).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA standard contrast ratio
- Keyboard navigable controls
- Respect prefers-reduced-motion
